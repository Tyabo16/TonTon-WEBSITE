iPhone 15 Pro Max

The iPhone 15 Pro Max represents the pinnacle of Apple's smartphone technology, featuring a stunning 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology. Crafted from premium titanium, it's both lighter and more durable than previous models.

Powered by the revolutionary A17 Pro chip, this device delivers unprecedented performance for mobile gaming, professional photography, and everyday tasks. The chip's enhanced Neural Engine enables advanced AI capabilities, while the 6-core GPU supports hardware-accelerated ray tracing for console-quality gaming.

Professional Camera System

Capture stunning photos and videos with the advanced camera system:

  • 48MP main camera with quad-pixel sensor
  • 12MP ultra wide camera with improved low-light performance
  • 12MP telephoto camera with 5x optical zoom
  • Front-facing TrueDepth camera with autofocus

All-day Battery Life

The iPhone 15 Pro Max features the best battery life ever in an iPhone, offering up to:

  • 29 hours of video playback
  • 25 hours of streamed video playback
  • 95 hours of audio playback

Fast charging capability allows you to charge up to 50% in around 30 minutes with a 20W adapter or higher (sold separately).
